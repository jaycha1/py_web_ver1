About this...

We are a non-profit organization that was founded mainly as a donation foundation to give children with unfortunate backgrounds free gear.

We will be receiving shoes, sport clothes, headbands, wristbands, and other gear related to playing baksetball as donations.

Values

MISSION: Our mission is to give every kid the opportunity to reach a higher level in their passions. We focus on giving children sufficient gear in order to play their favorite sports.

VISION: Our vision is that every child will be able to play sports.

What We Offer

DONEES: Donees simply have sign up to DONOR-HUB to receive free basketball gear such as shoes and clothes. We offer goods based on supplies, but we generally give once a month.

DONORS: Donors can donate their new or used gear in exchange for D-Tokens. With enough D-Tokens, they can be used to purchase new gear from our sponsor company.
